About Us

Nithya Jeya Raman

Welcome to my world of beauty and transformation! I'm Nithya, a Certified Makeup Artist with a passion for enhancing natural beauty and boosting confidence through the art of makeup. My journey in the makeup industry began with a deep love for creativity and self-expression, which led me to master the latest techniques and trends.

Whether it’s bridal, party, fashion, or everyday makeup, I specialize in creating flawless, customized looks that complement each individual's unique features. My goal is to ensure that every client feels radiant, empowered, and the best version of themselves.

Join me on this beautiful journey, and let’s create magic together!

My Certification from PLUSH Academy

I successfully completed my professional makeup artist certification at PLUSH Academy in Chennai, under the guidance of Santhosi Ma'am. She is an esteemed makeup artist and has worked with actors in the film industry.

During my training, I learned advanced makeup techniques, professional skills, and industry trends that helped shape my career. It was an incredible experience to be mentored by such a talented artist.
